Algorithm Optimization Function Benchmark Calculators

This page contains a number of calculators for different benchmark functions to measure the performance of optimization algorithms.

Background

I decided to make this page when I was working on my Particle Swarm Optimization project for my Nature-Inspired Computation class. Optimization problems typically involve finding a maximum or a minimum in a solution space. One way to measure the effectiveness of different algorithms for finding optima is by testing them on a battery of benchmark functions, in particular, functions that have many local optima and well-known global optima; finding the global optima demonstrates how good the algorithm is at converging to the global optima but also avoiding local optima.

Many of these optimization functions are very messy. In my Nature-Inspired Computation group's class, I struggled a bit with verifying the correctness of our function implementation because I could not find many online resources that implemented the functions. That is why I made this page.
